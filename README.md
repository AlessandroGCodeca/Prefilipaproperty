A private, automated real estate deal machine for Slovakia, built entirely for personal investment use.
The core idea is that every morning it scrapes thousands of Slovak property listings, runs them through a hard legal filter that kills anything with debt on the title deed, then scores what's left on whether the rental income covers all costs. You wake up to a curated shortlist of deals already classified and ranked — no manual searching.
The financial engine is the centrepiece. It runs every property through two simultaneous tax scenarios — buying personally versus through an s.r.o. — using 2026 Slovak rates including the critical 16% health levy that personal owners pay and companies don't. That single difference often flips a marginal deal into a profitable one.
The dashboard has four modules: a live deal feed showing only GREEN and YELLOW properties, a satellite viewer for visual vibe-checking without leaving the app, a tax toggle calculator you can run on any property in real time, and a one-click notary contract draft generator.
The longer-term vision includes a subscription layer where vetted investors pay €500/month to see the GREEN and YELLOW feed, and a middle-man spread model where you assign deals to buyers for a €10–20k fee without ever owning the property yourself — turning the data engine into a revenue stream.
